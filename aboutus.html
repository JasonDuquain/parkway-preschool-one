Values and mission statement needed

Who We Are: Parkway Presbyterian Preschool and Kindergarten, a private Christian preschool founded in 1999, currently serves 175 children ages 2 years through 5 years.  The program is accredited by the North Georgia Preschool Association and is recognized as a School of Excellence.

Where We Are: Parkway Presbyterian Preschool and Kindergarten is located on the campus of Parkway Presbyterian Church which has 16.6 acres of outdoor spaces filled with flowers, trees, and a garden that allows for investigation and learning experience. We are conveniently located off Bethleview Road and easily accessible to many areas of Forsyth County. Classrooms are age appropriate with centers and many options for exploration. 

Our Families:  Parents are an integral part of the program and are partners in the learning process. We invite parents into our preschool to serve and assist in many ways routinely such as the library and office, but often find many ways to assist within the classroom specifically to help the teacher. Parent/teacher conferences are held twice a year in addition to the daily and weekly communication between teachers and parents. Simply put, our Parkway families are the best and we consider it a high honor to partner in your child’s preschool years of learning and growth.

About: All staff at Parkway Presbyterian Preschool are CPR, First Aid, and Fire Safety Certified. They receive extensive background checks prior to employment and must complete 10 hours annually of training in Early Childhood Education. Many of our staff hold teaching degrees and hold several years of teaching experience.
