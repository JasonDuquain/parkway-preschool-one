All spots for the 2019-2020 school year have been filled. If you would like to add your child to a wait list, please send your child's name, date of birth, parent name, and good contact number to info@parkwaypreschool.com and we will add you. 
All new students will be required to complete a screening prior to full enrollment. You will be contacted by office personnel to schedule an appointment after your child has been placed.
The Forsyth County Schools Student Calendar can also be viewed by clicking this link.

Our school is open to all children in the community ages two years through five years by September 1st.  Enrollment is on a first come first serve basis with the following considerations:  Priority will be given to congregation members already in the program, then non-member families already in the program, siblings, alumni, congregation members, and public.  Children are admitted regardless of race, creed, color, gender, national origin, or religion.  Registration begins in early January each year for the upcoming year.
