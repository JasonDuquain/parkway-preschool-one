Our Staff

All staff at Parkway Presbyterian Preschool are CPR, First Aid, and Fire Safety Certified. They receive extensive background checks prior to employment and must complete 10 hours annually of training in Early Childhood Education. Many of our staff hold teaching degrees and hold several years of teaching experience.
