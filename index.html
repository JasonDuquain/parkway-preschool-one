Parkway Presbyterian Preschool and Kindergarten, a private Christian preschool founded in 1999, currently serves 175 children ages 2 years through 5 years. Our preschool is a ministry of Parkway Church, EPC. The program is accredited by the North Georgia Preschool Association and is recognized as a School of Excellence.

Our mission is to partner with parents to offer a loving, nurturing, and Christ-filled environment where each child can be encouraged to develop their unique personalities and God-given talents.
